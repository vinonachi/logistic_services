Land Transport

Our Core Areas


Cross-border Trucking


APEX has established agreements and collaborations with many countries in the TIR (Transport International Router) system. With fixed weekly departures, our cross-border trucking services provide controlled and predictable timing, ensuring shorter waiting times and faster transportation of goods.

  • Fixed trucking schedules and routes
  • Standard pricing
  • Ability to transport a wide range of goods, including lithium batteries and chemicals

TIR Trucking Services


Nationwide pickup service within the country

  • Nationwide pickup service within the country
  • Real-time tracking and monitoring of shipments
  • One-stop service at the border: customs clearance & loading/unloading
  • Cold chain transportation




Customized Transport Solutions
